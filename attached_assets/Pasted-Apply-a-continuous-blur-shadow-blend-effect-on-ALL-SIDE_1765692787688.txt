so that NO section separation is visible anywhere on the page.

DO NOT change:
- Text content
- Fonts
- Colors
- Navbar

- Buttons

================================================
GLOBAL BLUR & BLEND REQUIREMENT
================================================

There must be:
- NO visible horizontal separation
- NO visible vertical separation
- NO hard edges
- NO image cut lines

================================================
1. TOP BLEND (UP)
================================================

- At the TOP of the second section (where it meets the hero):
  - Apply a gradient + blur overlay.
  - Blend hero background INTO second section background.
  - No visible line where one ends and the other begins.

Implementation:
- Pseudo-element (::before)
- Height: 100–140px
- Gradient: transparent → dark
- Backdrop blur: 8px–12px

================================================
2. BOTTOM BLEND (DOWN)
================================================

- At the BOTTOM of the second section:
  - Apply a dark fade-out shadow.
  - Close the page visually without a hard stop.

Implementation:
- Pseudo-element (::after)
- Height: 100–140px
- Gradient: transparent → dark
- Optional blur: 6px–8px

================================================
3. LEFT & RIGHT BLEND (SIDES)
================================================

- Apply the SAME blur + shadow blending on BOTH LEFT and RIGHT edges
  of BOTH hero and second section.

Purpose:
- Remove vertical hard edges.
- Create a soft vignette / cinematic frame.
- Make background images feel infinite.

Implementation:
- Side overlays using ::before / ::after or wrapper elements.
- Width: 80–120px on each side.
- Gradient: transparent (center) → dark (edges).
- Blur: 6px–8px.

================================================
4. SECOND SECTION BACKGROUND SHADOW
================================================

- Add a subtle dark overlay to the second section background image.
- Improve text readability while keeping the image visible.

Target:
- Overlay opacity: 0.35–0.45
- Gradient preferred (lighter top → darker bottom).

================================================
5. FINAL RULES
================================================

- No borders
- No margins creating gaps
- No background color strips
- No visible seams anywhere

================================================
EXPECTED RESULT
================================================

- Hero and second section visually merge.
- Page feels like ONE continuous background.
- Soft blur vignette on all four sides.
- No separation is felt by the user.
